# Training Video Instructions

## Video Requirements
- **Format**: MP4 (primary) and WebM (fallback)
- **Duration**: 3-4 seconds (looping)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher (16:9 aspect ratio recommended)
- **File Size**: Keep under 10MB for optimal loading
- **Content**: Professional safety training scenes, people learning, safety equipment, etc.

## File Names
Place your video files in this directory with these exact names:
- `training-background.mp4` (primary video)
- `training-background.webm` (fallback video - optional)

## Video Content Suggestions
- People in safety gear during training
- Classroom training sessions
- Safety equipment demonstrations
- Professional safety consultants at work
- Certificates and safety documentation
- Industrial safety training scenarios

## Optimization Tips
- Compress video to reduce file size
- Use H.264 codec for MP4
- Keep video short (3-4 seconds) as it will loop
- Ensure good lighting and professional appearance
- Avoid copyrighted content or music

## Fallback
If no video is available, the hero section will display with a dark blue-grey background and overlay.
